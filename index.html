The Center of the Software Universe

GitHub's value extends to every corner of the development world. Its powerful network effect creates a virtuous cycle of innovation.

Open Source

The de facto home for open source. The frictionless "fork and pull" model democratized contribution, making it the public square for shared innovation and the world's largest library of code.

Individuals & Teams

A developer's public profile has become their modern resume and portfolio. For teams, it's a free, powerful platform for project management and collaboration from day one.

Enterprise

Combines the workflow developers love with the security, compliance, and governance large corporations need. Features like "InnerSource" break down silos and accelerate internal development.
